Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a young boy who learns on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He leaves his difficult life behind to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he forms deep friendships and discovers his past. As he grows, he repeatedly confronts the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who seeks power and immortality. Through challenges, losses, and moments of courage, Harry learns the value of loyalty, love, and sacrifice. The series follows his journey from innocence to maturity, showing how ordinary choices and strong friendships can shape one's destiny in extraordinary circumstances.
